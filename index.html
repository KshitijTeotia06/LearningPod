What are learning pods?

Learning pods are a new, safe way to allow kids to meet and study together. Enjoy the fun of school during COVID-19. Experience now using Learning Pods.

Who are we?

Supported from the Rishi Kumar for Congress campaign, Learning pods is a student run, non-profit grassroots organization dedicated to helping students learn and grow outside of the classroom. We realized because of the corona virus many students are not getting the learning opportunities they desired. Hence, learning pod stepped in. We have a fascination in tech and try to help our community everyday. We love our community and try to give back in all ways possible.
