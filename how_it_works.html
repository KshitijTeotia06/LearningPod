What are learning pods?

  • Learning pods are a new, safe way to allow kids to meet and study together.

  • Small groups that kids can meet in to study/play together in person or online

  • Groups are organized by grade level and proximity

  • Group size can range from 2-12 kids, depending on the number of students nearby/parent preferences




  • Kids can meet (while staying socially distanced) and work together on projects and school lessons

  • Also can do virtual learning pods (if they prefer that over in person)

  • This gives students a more in person learning experience that is OUTSIDE of the classroom, benefiting their social/emotional health as well as allowing them to have a study group or playgroup outside of just school
